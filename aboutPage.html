About Us
Our History
ERamen is one of the oldest ramen shops in the world. It was established since 1910 in Japan and brought to Indonesia since 2015. All ERamen menu made by organic flour and turn into delicious ramen noodle.
Our Vision
Our vision is to redefine the concept of ramen by preserving the authenticity of traditional recipes of Japanese flavors. We are committed to using only the finest, high-quality ingredients, such as organic flour for our noodles, ensuring a wholesome and exceptional dining experience.
Our Mission
Our mission is to provide nourishing culinary experiences that transcend expectations, immersing our guests in the authentic flavors and traditions of traditional ramen. We are dedicated to upholding the highest standards of quality, craftsmanship, and customer service, ensuring that every bowl of ramen is a masterpiece of taste and texture.
